NYT → Crossword Clash Bookmarklet

A bookmarklet that imports the New York Times crossword you're currently viewing into Crossword Clash so you can play it head-to-head with friends.

What this does: When clicked on a NYT crossword page, the bookmarklet fetches the puzzle from the NYT crossword API using your existing subscription cookie, converts it to Crossword Clash's format, and opens a new tab at crosswordclash.com with the puzzle loaded. It does not send your credentials anywhere else and requires an active NYT Crossword subscription. Full source is available on GitHub.

Install

Drag this button to your bookmarks bar:

NYT → Crossword Clash

Make sure your bookmarks bar is visible (Cmd+Shift+B on Mac, Ctrl+Shift+B on Windows).

Usage

  1. Visit a NYT crossword page — Daily, Mini, or any other puzzle — while logged into your NYT Crossword subscription.
  2. Click the bookmarklet in your bookmarks bar.
  3. Crossword Clash opens in a new tab with the puzzle pre-loaded.

How it works

  • Reads puzzle data from nytimes.com/svc/crosswords/v6/puzzle/... using the session cookie already in your browser.
  • Converts the puzzle into Crossword Clash's internal format and compresses it with lz-string.
  • Opens a new tab to crosswordclash.com and transfers the puzzle via window.postMessage.
  • No data is sent to any third-party server — the transfer is entirely between your two browser tabs.
